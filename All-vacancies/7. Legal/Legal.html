Legal / Senior Legal Counsel

Responsibilities

  • Review, draft and negotiate a wide variety of commercial contracts and policies including fintech contracts, technology, data and partnership agreements.
  • Take responsibility for the entire client contracting cycle – from initial draft to execution, working with business teams to drive contract negotiations to timely and successful completion;
  • Monitor, research and analyze existing law and changes in laws and/or statutes and regulations to evaluate and assess impact on the company and assist in developing necessary product or process changes;
  • Manage lawful preparation and circulation of procurement, human resources, financial, accounting and corporate documentation, ensure provision of legal advice in all aspects of company operations, cross-border activities and put forward effective proposals;
  • Perform other duties assigned by Chief Legal Officer.

Skills & Requirements

  • Bachelor degree in Law or International Law, UK or US LL.M. degree is an advantage.
  • 4 years’ experience in Law, at least 2 years of Contract Review/Drafting in English.
  • Fluency in Azerbaijani and English required, including a demonstrated skill to draft and negotiate contracts in these languages, Russian is an advantage.
  • Assertive and capable of handling and prioritizing multiple tasks
  • Excellent interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills with the ability to relate comfortably at all levels, with business clients and partners, and across borders, with minimal supervision.
